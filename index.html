Bondi Kitchen

Nestled in the corner of North Bondi, Bondi Kitchen is a refreshing sea breeze of modern Australian Cuisine, utilising foraged food found within a 10km radius. Organic produce and and fresh seafood is sourced on a daily basis to produce a varied and ecletic eating experience.

Our Story

Founded in 2008, we are pleased to soon reach our 10th anniversary. We are proud to have been one of the founders of the local foraging movement and to introduce sustainably sourced produce into a modern dining experience. Our crew of 10 is very hard working and enjoys bringing a slice of Bondi happiness to all customers. Enjoy!

Book Your Function With Us

With a capacity of 120 people over two linked dining areas (one with 40 and the other with 80 capacity), we are the perfect location for small or larger functions. Please provide a minimum of one weeks notice for a weekday or three weeks notice for a weekend booking. We are happy to customise requirements to your needs.
